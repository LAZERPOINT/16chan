What is Buggiechan?

Buggiechan is a image board based system that allows you to post pictures of your life or moments. The best part about Buggiechan is that you can be anonymous and don't have to create a account! On this website we have something called Boards, Boards are a way for people to find there favorite groups like Anime(/a/), Baseball(/b/), and Camping(/c/). We are working on more features to give you more customization (e.g. Threads, Video support, and more). So what are you waiting for? Try it now!
